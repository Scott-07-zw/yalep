Welcome to the Youth Advocacy Learning and Empowerment Platform (YALEP). Our mission is to provide adolecents and young people with realistic ,developmental and scientifical accurate SRH information.

Through this platform, you'll gain access to important lessons, assessments, and resources that will help you become an effective advocate for positive change in your community.

Start your learning journey today by exploring the lessons section, or learn more about our program in the info section.

We are committed to providing a safe and supportive learning environment for all our users.

YALEP is brought to you by Youth Advocates Zimbabwe, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young people with capabilities and oppotunities for healthy productive and fufilling lives.

Our programs are designed to be accessible, engaging, and impactful, helping you develop the skills and knowledge needed to make a difference.
