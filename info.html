The Youth Advocacy Learning and Empowerment Platform (YALEP) is an initiative by Youth Advocates Zimbabwe to provide SRH education to young people across the country.

Our programs focus on developing advocacy skills, leadership qualities, and community engagement techniques to empower the next generation of change-makers.

YALEP offers comprehensive courses covering various aspects of youth development, health education, and social advocacy.

We value your feedback! Share your experience with YALEP or ask any questions you may have.
